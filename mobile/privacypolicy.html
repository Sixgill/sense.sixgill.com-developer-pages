What This Privacy Policy Covers

This policy covers how Sixgill treats personal information that Sixgill collects and receives, including information related to Your use of the Sixgill Website(s), Your current and past use of Products and Services provided by Sixgill, directly or through third parties, and/or the Website and/or Application Stores authorized to carry Sixgill Products and Services.

Personal information is information about You that is personally identifiable like Your name, address, email address, or phone number, location, and device information, and that is not otherwise publicly available.

Sixgill may utilize information that it obtains from You to collect additional information about You from business partners or other companies and may combine information that it obtains from You with information from business partners or other companies.

This information may include, but not be limited to:

  • Website Information: When You register with Sixgill via one of the Sixgill Websites (www.sixgill.com), or when You use Sixgill Products and Services, You may choose to provide certain personally-identifiable information including, but not limited to Your name, email address, mobile phone number, LinkedIn account information, address, birth date, gender, cell phone service provider, places of interest to You, Your personal habits, occupation, industry, personal interests, relationship information, lifestyle information, social media interactions, and personal pictures/photographs. This policy only applies to websites owned or operated by Sixgill. This policy does not apply to any other websites or to the practices of companies that Sixgill does not own or control, or to people that Sixgill does not employ or manage. Sixgill Websites may contain links to third-party websites that are not owned or controlled by Sixgill. Sixgill has no control over, and assumes no responsibility for, the privacy policies or practices of any third-party websites. Information on such third-party websites may be obtained directly from those third-parties, possibly from their websites.

  • Registration Information: When You register with Sixgill and/or the Sixgill Website(s), and sign in to or use our services, directly or through third party applications or services, You are not anonymous to us. For certain services, we may request credit card or other payment account information which we maintain on secure servers. Sixgill collects information about Your transactions with us and with some of our business partners, including information about Your use of Products and Services that we offer. This information may include, but is not limited to any combination of phone number, email address, UDID, and/or device MAC address. Sixgill collects any attribute information that You provide that is relevant and/or required to offer Sixgill Products and Services.

  • Location Information: When You use Sixgill Products and Services Sixgill can collect location information and sensor data provided by Your device. This information may include, but is not limited to: GPS data (including, but not limited to device latitude/longitude, accuracy, heading, speed, and altitude), cell tower and Wi-Fi access point information.

  • Device Information: Sixgill automatically receives and records (collects) information from any device You use to view, register or download Sixgill Products and Services including, but not limited to, Your cell phone, tablet, PDA computer, personal computer, browser, car, appliances and M2M devices. Such records and information may include, but not be limited to, device-specific information, such as device UDID, device phone number, Your IP address, cookie information, software and hardware attributes, and the web page(s) You request.

  • Cookies and Website Data: Sixgill may set and access cookies on Your computer. A cookie is a small data string our server that writes to Your hard drive. This data string contains Your unique user ID for our Website. This technology also allows us to customize Your experiences when You visit the Sixgill Website(s). For example, we may be able to deliver content specific to Your interests, keep track of Your online ordering and alert You to new products.

Sixgill may let other companies that show advertisements on some of our pages set and access their cookies on Your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one. Advertisers or other companies may have access to Sixgill' cookies.

Sixgill collects non-personally identifiable information regarding Your visits to the Sixgill website(s). Cookies contain a session identification number that our systems use to recall previous authentication or order information from our servers. We may also collect Your domain name, IP address, the address of the last URL You visited prior to coming to our Website, and Your browser and platform type. We may use our or a third party navigation and localization services to target advertisements and content based on where You are located. We may use our or a third party tracking service to help us analyze this information in the aggregate to evaluate the effectiveness of our site, Products and Services. In some cases, the information is collected directly by the third party and in others it is forwarded by Sixgill to the third party.

A cookie cannot be used to access or otherwise compromise the data on Your hard drive. Your privacy is always protected. However, at any time You may choose to change Your browser settings to disable cookies if You do not want us to establish and maintain a unique website user ID for You. Please be aware that cookies may be required to complete certain functions on the Sixgill Web site(s), such as ordering online. Please consult the instructions provided by Your browser provider to change Your browser settings, remove cookies or to disable cookies.

Sixgill uses information for the following general purposes to: provide services that You activate or for other Products and Services, improve our services, contact You, conduct research, provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients, and for sale to other clients or third parties in non-aggregated and aggregated forms (but only as set forth below).

The only way to prevent Sixgill from collecting personal information about You is to not use our Website, Products, Services and/or software, directly or through our business partners and other third parties who use our Products and Services as part of their offerings. Sixgill retains some historical personal information obtained through Your use of Sixgill Products and Services even after their removal from Your devices. (See "Discontinuing Use of Sixgill Products and Services")

  • Sixgill reiterates that Sixgill, the Website(s) and all Sixgill Products and Services are not designed or intended to attract children under the age of 13 as users and Sixgill does not intentionally collect personal information from such children. However, if a parent/guardian permits their child under age 13 to register with Sixgill, to use the Website, and/or to use Sixgill Products and Services, then that parent/guardian is permitting Sixgill to collect and use their child's personal information in the same manner set forth herein that Sixgill uses any other user's personal information.

  • Sixgill responds to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend ourselves against legal claims.

  • Sixgill believes it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of the Sixgill Terms and Conditions of Use, or as otherwise required by law.

  • Sixgill may disclose or transfer the information upon the closing of a definitive agreement under which a majority of the outstanding stock or assets of Sixgill are acquired by a 3rd party.

  • Sixgill may display targeted advertisements and content based on Your personal information. Advertisers (including advertisement serving companies) may assume that people who interact with, view, or click targeted advertisements meet the targeting criteria, for example, women ages 18-24 from a particular geographic area. However, not all advertisements and content may be appropriate for You. If You disapprove of advertisements or content targeted to You, Your only choice to prevent further such advertisements or content being shown to You is to discontinue use of the Sixgill Website(s), Products and Services.

  • Sixgill may disclose location information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to (a) conform to a legal order or comply with legal process served on Sixgill; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Sixgill; or (c) act in urgent circumstances to protect the health and safety of any persons.
Sixgill does not notify its users when their personal and/or location information has been disclosed to partners or 3rd parties.
